About Musclecars

What is a muscle car? What makes a muscle car, a muscle car? According to experts online, a muscle car is really just a term used to describe any wide varity of powerful, high-performance vehicle. According to Experts online, a muscle car is any car that has more horsepower. This means, if it has more horsepower than another car, it might be one. If it has V8 or higher on the engine, it might be a muscle car. There aren't as many around as there used to be-atleast well-known ones.
